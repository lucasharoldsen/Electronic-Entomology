orchid_Danny is a itteration of orchid_0. It waits around slowly clicking and tweets. Lower the light it gets longer it waits to tweet and longer it sings per intervals. Calls are different from time to time. Using uncut components and soldering them on a hoops makes a curly hoops like an orchid pedals.

orchid_Danny lives at Danny's Pizza where it greets the costomers with its chirps.
