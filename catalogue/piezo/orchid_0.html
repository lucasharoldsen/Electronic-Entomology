Orchid_0 tweets in a intervals. It waits around slowly clicking and tweets. Lower the light it gets longer it waits to sweet and longer it sings per intervals. Calls are different from time to time. Using uncut components and soldering them on a hoops makes a curly hoops like an orchid pedals.

There are two component to this circuit. One is a pulse generator and the other is for the voice.
