74HC14 Schmitt Trigger Inverter --- data sheet

Many of bugs from this specie use common CMOS(Complementary Metal Oxide Semiconductor) chip called Schmitt Trigger Inverter. CMOS chips are logic IC(integrated circuit) chip family that has premade miniature circuit in them. Schmitt Trigger is often used to convert complex analog signals to digital logic. Basically, it can look at an input voltage and when the input is higher than the threshold, it can say the logic is HIGH. It can also say logic is LOW when the input voltage is below the threshold. So it can recognize the change of voltage in a binary fashion. Finally, it is also an inverter circuit which means that if the input voltage goes above the threshold it registers logic as LOW instead of HIGH.

There are 6 independent schmitt trigger inverter circuits in 74HC14 chip. By bridging input and output of the inverter with resistors and pulling input to ground, you can create an oscilator.
